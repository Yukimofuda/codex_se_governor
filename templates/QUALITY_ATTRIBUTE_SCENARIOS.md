## Scenario Format

- Attribute: quality concern such as performance, reliability, security, usability, maintainability, compatibility, compliance, scalability, or resilience.
- Stimulus: event that triggers the scenario.
- Environment: normal, degraded, attack, peak load, deployment, maintenance, or failure context.
- Response: system behavior expected.
- Response Measure: measurable target such as time, error rate, coverage, recovery time, or review evidence.
- Trade-off: quality attribute that may get worse.

## Required Review Questions

- Is the response measure testable or reviewable?
- Which requirement or risk does this scenario trace to?
- Which test or monitoring evidence will verify it?
- What rollback or contingency exists if the measure is not met?
